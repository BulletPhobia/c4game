How to Play

Game Objective

You are an event organizer who must plan successful, sustainable events while managing your budget. Your goal is to level up by gaining experience points (XP) from successfully organizing events.

Key Concepts

Sustainability Rating

Each event needs at least a Bronze rating (50%) to succeed. Your sustainability rating is based on the items you choose for your event.

Rating Levels:

Platinum 95% or above

Gold 90% or above

Silver 75% or above

Bronze 50% or above

Below 50%: Fail

Requirements

Each event has specific items you must include. For example, a workshop might require tables, chairs, and craft supplies. You must include items that satisfy all requirements to succeed.

Budget

Trying to stay within budget will maximize your XP earnings. Going over budget will reduce your XP, but sometimes it's necessary to achieve high sustainability ratings.

Quality Investment

You can invest extra money to improve the quality of your event, which increases attendee ratings but costs more.

Gameplay

  1. You'll be assigned an event with specific requirements, budget, and expected attendees.
  2. Read the event description and requirements carefully.
  3. Purchase necessary items to meet the requirements from the available options.
  4. Adjust quality investment to improve attendee ratings.
  5. Balance your spending with sustainability ratings.
  6. Finish planning to see your results.
  7. Successful events earn you XP and profit.
  8. Level up to unlock bigger events with larger budgets.

Tips

  • Items with higher sustainability ratings cost more but help you achieve better sustainability scores.
  • Always check if you've satisfied all requirements before finishing.
  • Try to balance quality and sustainability - both affect your final XP.
  • As you level up, save money for bigger events that will require more expensive items.
