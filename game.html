        How to Play

        Game Objective

        You are an event organizer who must plan successful, sustainable events while managing your budget.

        Key Concepts

        • Sustainability Rating: Each event needs at least a Bronze rating (50%) to succeed.
        • Ratings:
          • Platinum: 95% or above
          • Gold: 90% or above
          • Silver: 75% or above
          • Bronze: 50% or above
          • Below 50%: Fail
        • Requirements: Each event has specific items you must include.
        • Budget: Trying to stay within budget will maximize your XP earnings.

        Gameplay

        1. Read the event description and requirements carefully.
        2. Purchase necessary items to meet the requirements.
        3. Adjust quality investment to improve attendee ratings.
        4. Balance your spending with sustainability ratings.
        5. Complete events to earn XP and level up for bigger events.

        Tips

        Items with higher sustainability ratings cost more but help you achieve better sustainability scores.
